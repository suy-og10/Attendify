About Attendify

The intelligent approach to face recognition attendance mapping.

The Project

Attendify is an AI-powered Attendance Management System designed to streamline the academic attendance process. By leveraging advanced facial recognition technology, Attendify eliminates proxy attendance, drastically reduces manual administrative burden, and provides deep analytical insights into student participation.

Our system is built with secure role-based access to empower Students, Teachers, HODs, and Administrators with the tailored visibility they need.
